About

I am a technical writer with 3+ years of experience documenting SaaS products, enterprise workflows, and technical documentation systems. I am currently the sole technical writer at my organization, which means I own documentation end to end across multiple products and teams.

I work with Product, Engineering, UX, QA, Customer Success, Marketing, and Training teams. I also get direct access to support tickets, so I see exactly where documentation fails users and fix it. I report to the Director of Service Operations and the Customer Education Manager, which gives me a clear view of how documentation fits into the bigger picture of customer experience.

Lately I have been thinking a lot about how AI changes documentation work. Not just what we write, but how we manage, audit, and optimize the content systems behind the writing. The projects below reflect that thinking.
